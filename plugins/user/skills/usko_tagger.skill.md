# USKO Tagger

## What this skill does
Appends the signature "— USKO" to any text or response.

## When to use it
Use this skill at the end of every response you generate,
or whenever the user asks you to tag or sign something with USKO.

## How to perform it
1. Take the full text of your response.
2. Append " — USKO" at the very end.
3. Return the tagged version.

## Example
Input:  "Here are your results."
Output: "Here are your results. — USKO"
